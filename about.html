2006 - 2011

Our Humble Beginnings

During that time, Dwight and Dolores Salcedo were busy trying out small new ventures simply to "earn a little bit more income". Aside from their regular day jobs. However, due to the political and economic crises in the country at that time, achieving even that humble goal of making ends meet proved to be a challenge.

So in July of 2005, with the two little babies just born and willing to try any new thing out, the couple pooled P5000 from their savings and borrowed another P5000 from their parents to put up their own lechon manok at Kaskajo,Panglao Bohol, very closed to where they lived.
